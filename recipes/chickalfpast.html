Chicken Alfredo Pasta

Description

Chicken Alfredo Pasta is a culinary indulgence, a harmony of flavors and textures that captivates the palate. Tender pieces of seasoned chicken, sautéed to perfection, intertwine with al dente fettuccine,
creating a symphony of comfort. The creamy Alfredo sauce, a velvety blend of heavy cream and Parmesan cheese, blankets the pasta, infusing each bite with richness. Accented by the subtle bite of garlic
and the aromatic allure of fresh parsley, this dish is a celebration of simplicity and sophistication. Whether enjoyed for a cozy dinner or shared with loved ones, Chicken Alfredo Pasta is a timeless classic
that never fails to delight.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz fettucine pasta
  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Steps

  1. Cook Pasta:
    Boil fettuccine pasta in salted water until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. Cook Chicken:
    Season chicken pieces with salt and pepper. In a pan, heat olive oil over medium-high heat. Add chicken and cook until browned and cooked through. Remove chicken from the pan and set aside.
  3. Prepare Alfredo Sauce:
    In the same pan, add minced garlic and sauté briefly. Pour in heavy cream, stirring continuously. Add Parmesan cheese gradually, stirring until the sauce thickens. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Combine Chicken and Pasta:
    Add the cooked chicken back to the pan, allowing it to coat in the Alfredo sauce. Stir in the cooked fettuccine pasta, ensuring it's well-coated with the sauce.
  5. Serve:
    Plate the Chicken Alfredo Pasta, garnish with chopped fresh parsley, and sprinkle additional Parmesan if desired. Serve hot.
